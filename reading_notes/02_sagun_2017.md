## Why this paper second

Builds intuition for what Hessians actually look like in practice — bulk + outliers structure. Foundation for Theorem 1.

## What to focus on while reading

1. **The "bulk + outliers" observation** (Section 3) — this is the key empirical fact you need.
2. **Their explanation** — why does the structure emerge? Note the role of parameter count vs data complexity.
3. **The implication for conditioning** — large outliers = small directions of high curvature = bad conditioning.
